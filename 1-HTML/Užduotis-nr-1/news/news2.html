CodeCamp Expands with New Office in San Francisco

We are excited to announce that CodeCamp is expanding with the opening of a new office in San Francisco. This new location will help us better serve our growing community of learners on the West Coast and offer a variety of new opportunities for in-person events, workshops, and networking.

Why San Francisco?

San Francisco, being one of the largest tech hubs in the world, is the perfect place for our expansion. It allows us to be closer to the heart of innovation and to foster connections with industry leaders, startups, and tech professionals who are eager to share their expertise with our learners.

What the New Office Offers:

  • In-Person Events: Our new office will host regular in-person coding workshops, bootcamps, and hackathons to bring the CodeCamp community together in a collaborative environment.
  • Exclusive Workshops: Learners can now attend exclusive, on-site workshops led by industry professionals from top tech companies based in the Bay Area.
  • Networking Opportunities: The new office will offer learners the chance to connect with tech recruiters, developers, and entrepreneurs, helping them grow their professional network.
  • Career Development Support: In-person career counseling and portfolio review sessions will be available to support our learners as they prepare for jobs in tech.

Grand Opening Event

To celebrate the opening of our San Francisco office, we will be hosting a Grand Opening Event next month. The event will feature keynote speakers from leading tech companies, networking sessions, and coding workshops. Stay tuned for more details!
